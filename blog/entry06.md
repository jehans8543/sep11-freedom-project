#### Slides 
Every year we have to make slides for our freedom project. And every year Mr. Mueller gives us a lesson on how our slides should look like. Our slides are part of our presentation grade, since it's what our audience looks at when we give our presentation. Here are the basic requirements for our slides:

* Not too much text on the screen
* Images/visuals to display what you are talking about
* Text can be seen from all the way from the back of the room
